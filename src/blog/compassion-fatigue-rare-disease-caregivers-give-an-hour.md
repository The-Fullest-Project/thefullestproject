## Compassion Fatigue in Rare Disease Caregivers

Caregivers of individuals with rare diseases face unique challenges that can lead to compassion fatigue — the emotional, physical, and psychological toll of providing ongoing care. Give an Hour's dedicated program for rare disease caregivers offers peer support, free mental health resources, and strategies for building resilience.
