We're so excited to officially launch The Fullest Project.

As caregivers ourselves, we know firsthand how isolating and overwhelming it can feel to navigate the world of disability resources. You hear about a great program from another parent at therapy. You stumble across a foundation through a random Google search at 2 AM. You find out about equipment loaner programs only because someone in your support group mentioned them.

**What about those who don't have those connections?**

That question is at the heart of everything we do. The Fullest Project exists to be the centralized hub that we wish existed when we first started our journeys as caregivers.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Resource Directory** — A searchable, filterable directory of local and national resources for individuals with disabilities and their caregivers.
- **Therapy Guide** — Clear explanations of different therapy types and when each is most beneficial.
- **Adaptive Equipment** — Equipment categories, loaner programs, and curated product recommendations.
- **School & IEP Navigation** — Your rights, the IEP process, and advocacy tips to help you navigate the education system.
- **Community** — Stories, blog posts, and eventually our podcast — because none of us should do this alone.

## We Need Your Help

This directory is only as good as the resources in it. If you know of a program, service, or organization that supports individuals with disabilities — [submit it](/submit-resource/). Every resource you add helps another family find what they need.

## Stay Connected

Sign up for our newsletter to get updates on new resources, blog posts, and community events. We're just getting started, and we're so glad you're here.

Here's to living life to the fullest.

*— Erin & Nicole*
